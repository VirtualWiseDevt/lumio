   Step 1 - Confirm:
   - Shows plan name and price: "You're subscribing to [Monthly] for KES [1,250]"

   - On success: move to step 2, begin polling
   - On error: show error toast, re-enable button

   Step 2 - Waiting:
   - "Check your phone" message with phone number displayed
   - Animated spinner (use Motion for rotation or CSS animation)
   - "A payment prompt has been sent to [phone]. Enter your M-Pesa PIN to complete the payment."
   - Amount reminder: "KES [1,250]"
   - Call pollPaymentStatus(paymentId) from billing API
   - On SUCCESS: move to step 3 with result="success"
   - On FAILED: move to step 3 with result="failed"
   - On timeout: move to step 3 with result="timeout"

   Step 3 - Result:
   - Success: green check icon, "Payment Successful!", receipt number, "Your [Monthly] subscription is now active until [date]". "Done" button closes modal and calls onSuccess.
   - Failed: red X icon, "Payment Failed", result description, "Try Again" button (resets to step 1 with same plan and phone). "Close" button.
